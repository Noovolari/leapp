Noovolari has officially come to an end.

We have decided to close down Noovolari.

This decision, taken after careful evaluation and reflection, marks the end of a journey embarked upon with ambition and passion to evolve into a self-sustaining Startup.

Regarding the open-source project Leapp, we assure you that it will continue to exist, with a scaled-down level of support and updates.

The Pro and Team versions will remain online until 30 June to facilitate the transition to other solutions. We remain available to assist you with specific needs. Don't hesitate to get in touch with us at info@leapp.cloud
